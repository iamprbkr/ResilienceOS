# UI Wireframes

## Command Center

First screen shows live exercise status, resilience score, elapsed time, role count, attack map, critical injects, standards coverage, and executive decisions. This is the default operating surface for a CISO or exercise director.

## Scenario Builder

Builder presents reusable scenario blueprints, selected standards, objectives, roles, duration, difficulty, and AI generation controls. Production version should support branching rules, inject timeline authoring, attachments, dependency maps, and approval workflow.

## Inject Console

Injects are grouped by role and channel: SOC, Executive, Regulator, Media, AI Governance, Cloud Ops. Facilitators can approve AI-generated injects, pause delivery, mutate pressure, and record observation notes.

## Standards Navigator

Graph surface connects techniques, controls, standards, assets, and business services. Users can filter by MITRE ATT&CK, ATLAS, D3FEND, OWASP, NIST, ISO, DORA, GDPR, DPDP, CSA, WEF, and WHO guidance.

## After-Action Workspace

Report workspace includes executive summary, timeline, decisions, evidence, control gaps, maturity scoring, standards coverage, regulator-ready appendix, and board-level recommendations.
