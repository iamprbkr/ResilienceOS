The Solution

One platform. Unlimited exercises. Measurable readiness.

ResilienceOS replaces the annual consultancy cycle with continuous, adaptive simulation. Every exercise produces a score that trends over time. Every decision maps to regulatory frameworks. Every report exports as auditor-ready evidence.

01

Adaptive Injects

Scenarios react to decisions made during the exercise. Choose to contain rather than pay? The next inject tests your containment procedures. No two runs produce the same outcome.

02

Standards Mapping

Every exercise maps to NIST CSF 2.0, ISO 27001, SOC 2, MITRE ATT&CK, MITRE ATLAS, AI RMF, ISO 42001, and DORA. Coverage reports show gaps across frameworks simultaneously.

03

Evidence Factory

After-action reports generate as PDF, DOCX, or CSV. Board-ready evidence from live simulation, not self-assessment. Audit trail with search and filters.

04

Multi-Tenant SaaS

RBAC with 15 roles and 5 permissions. 15 workspace views: Command, Tabletop, Scenarios, Threat AI, Training, Standards, Reports, Admin, and more. Light and dark mode.
